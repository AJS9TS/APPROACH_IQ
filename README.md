# APPROACH IQ

**Precision Practice Intelligence for the Modern Golfer**

---

Stop guessing. Start improving.

APPROACH IQ is the golf practice app that turns mindless range sessions into data-driven breakthroughs. No subscriptions. No accounts. No excuses. Just open it, hit shots, and watch the patterns emerge.

### What it does

You hit a shot. You log where it landed. APPROACH IQ does the rest.

- **See your misses** on a visual green canvas — instantly spot whether you pull left, leak right, or come up short
- **Know your numbers** with per-club KPIs benchmarked against pro, good amateur, and average levels
- **Get a practice plan** generated from YOUR data — not generic YouTube advice
- **Track your handicap** and see whether your range work is actually translating to the course
- **Log at speed** with Quick Log mode — two taps per shot, designed for mid-round use without slowing down play

### How it works

One HTML file. Zero setup. Works offline. Install it to your home screen and it feels like a native app.

**On the range:** Use Click-to-Place mode to tap exactly where each ball finishes relative to your target. Or type the numbers manually if you're old school.

**On the course:** Quick Log mode. Tap your club, tap the zone. Done. The app randomises coordinates within each zone so your dispersion data stays realistic.

**After your session:** Hit the Coach's Corner tab. See which clubs are letting you down, why, and exactly what to work on next time.

### The stack

- Single HTML file, vanilla JS, no dependencies beyond Chart.js for trend lines
- localStorage for everything — your data never leaves your device
- PWA with service worker — works offline after first load
- Responsive from phone to ultrawide
